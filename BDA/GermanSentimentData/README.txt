This data collection contains 500 opinionated texts from social media about German celebrities, focusing on German singers and musicians. Each text is annotated by three annotators in term of its sentiment and it is assigned both positive and negative labels. 

The data collection is licensed under LGPL for Linguistic Resources and it is freely available. All rights of the provided annotations are reserved for the Hasso Plattner Institute and the Celebrity Performance Indexing Company.

If the data collection is used in a research and the outcome of the research is published, the user of the data is kindly requested to cite this paper:
Saeedeh Momtazi, "Fine-grained German Sentiment Analysis on Social Media", In Proceedings of the International Conference on Language Resources and Evaluation (LREC), 2012.
